How to Win Every PayPal Dispute

If a buyer has charged back a payment, PayPal’s policy states that they do not cover parties when intangible items are purchased. This can be used to your advantage, with careful wording.

Therefore, I deserve to receive my rightfully owned money, and the buyer should be looked into. Intangible items are not covered under your buyer protection policy.

Method 3: Chargeback an Item

If you purchased a virtual item and would like to charge back the buyer, make it seem as if you have purchased a tangible item. PayPal will have no choice but to use Buyer Protection and award your money back. The seller will not have a tracking number for the virtual item, and you will automatically win.

Hello sir/madame!
I was just wanting to know where my Headphones (INSERT ANY PRODUCT HERE, THAT FITS THE PRICE YOU PAID) are. It's been a little over 20 days.. now and I haven't received it at my house yet! If you could just send me a tracking number, I'll cancel this case immediately! Thanks! (YOUR NAME)

Method 4: Open Unauthorized Access Case

If you purchased a good and convince PayPal that this transaction was unauthorized, then the money will be awarded to you. For this to work, you must change your security questions and password before the case is filed.

I believe that someone has gained remote access to my computer. Unauthorized payments were sent as well through PayPal. My computer just came from the virus removal center, and I would like help to receive my money back. I have also changed my password and security questions for further safety. Please help me receive the full amount that was sent with unauthorized access.
