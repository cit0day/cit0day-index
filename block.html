3. Entering Recovery Mode

To start the process, you need to put your iPhone into recovery mode. This varies slightly depending on the iPhone model:

iPhone 8 or later

  1. Connect your iPhone to the computer.
  2. Press and quickly release the Volume Up button.
  3. Press and quickly release the Volume Down button.
  4. Press and hold the side button until you see the recovery mode screen.

iPhone 7 and 7 Plus

  1. Connect your iPhone to the computer.
  2. Press and hold both the Volume Down and side buttons simultaneously.
  3. Continue holding them until you see the recovery mode screen.

iPhone 6s and earlier

  1. Connect your iPhone to the computer.
  2. Press and hold both the Home and top (or side) buttons simultaneously.
  3. Continue holding them until you see the recovery mode screen.
